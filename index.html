What is SafeHelp?

SafeHelp is a cutting-edge emergency response app designed to save lives in high-risk situations. Whether you're under attack, in an accident, or being harassed, SafeHelp is always listening—ready to call for help when you can’t.

It acts as your automated lifeline, bridging the gap between an incident and the arrival of help.

Why SafeHelp?

Because every second counts. Many victims of violence or accidents are unable to make a call or reach for their phones. Delays in response can have devastating consequences.

SafeHelp bypasses these barriers with automation, precision, and urgency, providing critical information instantly to accelerate help.

How Does It Work?

Trigger

Simply say your chosen safe word (e.g., "help") or press a keyboard shortcut (like Ctrl) when in distress.

Action

Instantly sends live location, starts a secure video feed, & places an automated call to emergency services.

Backup

Securely saves location/video & optionally sends alerts to pre-selected trusted contacts or authorities.

Have Questions? We Have Answers.

Find clarity on how SafeHelp works and protects your data.

Does Safe Help work without internet?

Some features like SMS location alerts and direct hotline calls (if cellular service is available) can work offline. However, real-time location tracking updates and live video streaming require an active internet connection (Wi-Fi or mobile data).

What if I trigger it by accident?

Don't worry! You'll have a brief window (typically 5 seconds, configurable in settings) to cancel a false trigger before any alerts or calls are initiated. Just tap the cancel button on screen.

Can I choose who gets my alert?

Yes, absolutely. In the app settings, you can designate specific trusted contacts (family, friends, neighbors) who will receive emergency alerts including your location and a link to the live video feed (Pro plan) when Safe Help is activated.

Is my data secure?

Protecting your privacy and data is our top priority. We use end-to-end encryption for communications and secure, compliant cloud storage for video and location data. Access is strictly controlled.

How is Safe Help different from just calling 911?

Safe Help excels in speed, automation, and providing crucial context. It can be triggered hands-free when you might be unable to dial. It instantly provides live video and precise location, giving responders immediate situational awareness, which can significantly improve response effectiveness compared to a voice-only call.
